### my history

Prepare for the long and maybe even slightly interesting journey through my years of yore.
Well, I started with... Minecraft. Yes, I remember it clearly. It was when 12 year old me wanted to make a smelting furnace recipe of converting coal into diamond.
I have a hunch that it involved extracting a jar archive, navigating to Recipes.class or something and in a wordpad changing a value from Items.wood to Items.diamond (certainly different names). I recall my happiness when after several hours I was finally greeted with possibility of baking diamonds.
And so I jumped onto the track of Java programming. Proppeled by my desire of Minecraft Modding and many awesome Youtube tutorials which were already present at that time I came to know some coffee here. I had made some progress, ended up posting even simple tutorials on how to add new blocks and link them together to create portals. (Without any special graphic treatment unsurprisingly. My greatest graphical ? consisted of salt_block_texture). Sincere thanks to Forge guys here, they made a spectacular MinecraftModdingAPI.
